Rules
8

Participation

Anyone under 21 who does not have a high school diploma may compete.

Teams

Teams should not have more than five people.

Outside Help

You cannot recieve help from humans outside the competition, although you are free to use whatever inanimate resources you like (wikipedia, stackOverflow, etc). You may recieve help from other teams, but your hackathon project may not depend on theirs.

Prior Work

You are free to use third-party libraries or your own prior work. However, it must be clear what you produced at the hackathon and what you did not. Only code produced at the hackathon will be judged.

Behavior

You must comply with all instructions given by organisers.
